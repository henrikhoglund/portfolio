Falsterbo Horseshow - 2015

Falsterbo Horsehow is one of the biggest outdoor festivals in Sweden with over 60.000 visitors. Their existing app was outdated and needed a makeover. One of our major challenges was the lack of iOS programming skills, since none of us had worked with this language.

Throughout the project, we worked with the Minimum Viable Product in mind. The focus was to create a clear visual design that the user would recognize instantly. We had the core focus creating a great looking, stable App with offline compatibility. To create more engagement with the visitors, we added a section for social media.
